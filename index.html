Welcome to Kerala School of Mathematics

The Kerala School of Mathematics (KSoM) is an initiative by the Government of Kerala to foster high-level research and advanced education in mathematics. Located in Kozhikode, KSoM aims to be a leading center for mathematical sciences, attracting talent from across the nation and beyond.

Our mission is to create a vibrant academic environment that promotes cutting-edge research, nurtures young mathematicians, and contributes significantly to the global mathematical community.
