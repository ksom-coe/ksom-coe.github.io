About KSoM

Kerala School of Mathematics (KSoM) is an advanced research institute in Mathematics which started in 2007 as a joint venture of the Department of Atomic Energy (DAE), Government of India and the Science and Technology Department, Government of Kerala. KSoM was founded with the vision of establishing a centre of excellence for research in Mathematics and to revive the glory of a school by a similar name which flourished between the fourteenth and sixteenth century in Kerala.

The main objective of Kerala School of Mathematics is conduct of high quality Mathematical Research. Our mission is to create a vibrant academic environment that promotes cutting-edge research, nurtures young mathematicians, and contributes significantly to the global mathematical community.

Research Areas at KSoM

Kerala School of Mathematics conducts pioneering research across fundamental and applied mathematics, fostering interdisciplinary collaborations that advance mathematical frontiers. Our research groups explore deep structures in complex systems, geometric topologies, analytical frameworks, and number-theoretic phenomena, contributing to both theoretical foundations and emerging applications.

Complex Analysis
Investigating Teichmüller theory, Hörmander's estimates on Bers domains, and geometric implications of Kobayashi metrics. Braid & Mapping Class Groups
Examining topological structures through liftability problems, Birman-Hilden theory, and quantum representations. Harmonic Analysis
Studying time-frequency methods, modulation spaces, and nonlinear PDEs in nilpotent Lie groups. Non-commutative Functional Analysis
Advancing operator space theory with applications to quantum approximation and convexity. Analytic Number Theory
Developing innovative techniques for prime distributions, zeta values, and arithmetic structures.

Academic Programmes at KSoM

Kerala School of Mathematics offers research-intensive academic pathways designed to cultivate exceptional mathematical talent. Our programmes blend rigorous coursework with cutting-edge investigation, providing dedicated mentorship, comprehensive financial support, and access to world-class facilities within a collaborative community.

Integrated M.Sc.-Ph.D.
Seamless five-year progression with specializations in Mathematics, Physics, or Theoretical Computer Science. Ph.D. Programme
Doctoral candidates pursue advanced research under expert faculty guidance, culminating in a dissertation. Postdoctoral Fellowships
Two-year renewable positions with stipends, research grants, and development support.
