Welcome to the Kerala School of Mathematics

An autonomous institution established as a joint venture of the Science and Technology Department, Government of Kerala, and the Department of Atomic Energy, Government of India.

About KSoM

The Kerala School of Mathematics (KSoM) is an advanced research institute in Mathematics, established in 2009 as a joint venture of the Department of Atomic Energy (DAE) and the Kerala State Council for Science, Technology and Environment (KSCSTE). The institute aims to provide a platform for excellent mathematicians from India and abroad to engage in high-quality research and academic activities.

Academic Programs

  • Integrated MSc-PhD Program: An advanced training program at the postgraduate level, offering a seamless transition from Master's to Doctoral studies in Mathematics.
  • Ph.D. Program: A research-intensive program aimed at fostering original contributions to various fields of Mathematics.
  • Post-Doctoral Fellowships: Opportunities for young researchers to engage in independent research across diverse areas of Mathematics.

Research Areas

KSoM hosts research groups in various subfields of Mathematics, including:

  • Analytic Number Theory
  • Algebra
  • Geometry
  • Topology
  • Mathematical Physics
  • Applied Mathematics
